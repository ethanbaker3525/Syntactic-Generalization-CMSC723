**Data Organization (by folder)**

*original_howitt_data*:
- contains all sentences generated by Howitt et al
- files with *c* are related to Howitt's evaluation set. Howitt's Eval set had: 486 clefting, 486 topicalization with intro, 161 topicalization without intro, and 243 tough movement. Files with *c* have x4 these numbers. 
- files with *i* seem to contain all the unused sentences. 
- *c* files and *i* files do not share items.
- the criticalwords feild indicates words at which to evaluate model suprisal.
- conditions: a/x refers to the presence/absence of a filler, b/x refers to the presence/absence of a gap.
	- ab = +filler +gap (grammatical)
	- xb = -filler +gap (ungrammatical)
	- ax = +filler -gap (ungrammatical)
	- xx = -filler -gap (grammatical)

*processed_wilcox_data*:
- contains wilcox's WH-movement data, with Howitt's preprocessing. 

*note.* "_" represent theoretical gaps, and should not be used as tokens in model inputs/outputs
